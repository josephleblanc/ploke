# Archive Boundary

Archive old lab-book entries when they are no longer part of the current working narrative.

## Keep In Current

- the recent sequence of work needed to understand the active direction
- entries still being referenced in current EDRs or follow-up tasks
- recent surprising failures or reversals that are still shaping decisions

## Move To Archive

- entries whose follow-up is complete
- superseded narrative threads that no longer affect current planning
- old daily notes that remain useful historically but no longer need to stay in the main reading path

## Rule

Archiving is for chronology only. Do not delete history, and do not move durable beliefs or durable decisions here from the evidence ledger or EDRs.
